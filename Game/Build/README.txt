You start in a prison cell with a knife and a guard. Kill the guard, take his gun and kill some Nazis on your way out.
You win the game by using the lever at the end of the level.

Controls
	Escape: Toggle cursor visibility
	9: Lower mouse sensitivity
	0: Increase mouse sensitivity

    (gameplay)
    Move: WASD
    Look: Mouse
    Attack: Space or Left Mouse
    Use: E

    (free camera only)
    Hold Left Shift: Fly faster
    Q: Fly up 
    E: fly down

    (cheats)
    1: Select game camera
    2: Select free camera (The free camera is a different camera and is not recognized by the game. The enemies will still try to shoot the player where the normal camera is.)
    3: Toggle collider wireframe
    4: Toggle god mode (can't get hit by enemies and can go through walls)
    5: Reset game

Known bugs
    Sometimes health is not displayed.
